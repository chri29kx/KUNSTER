KUNSTER

Katrine Hvid

My field of work is abstract and often approaches a formal minimalistic expression. I work in a non-narrative, materialbased field, and I am interested in the tangibility in the material and the intangible, that working with the material gives back. Important words in the workflow are responsiveness, intuition, patience and perfection, and there is a necessity for a room for contemplation. I think about how everything is changeable and yet always stays the same. It is always composed of the same elements and yet is experienced differently each second. For this reason, working with repetition and variation makes sense to me.
